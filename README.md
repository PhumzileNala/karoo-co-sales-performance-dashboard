# 📊 Karoo & Co. Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyses Karoo & Co.'s 2025 sales performance using Microsoft Power BI.

The objective was to analyse sales performance across regions, products, customer segments, sales channels and marketing campaigns, and provide actionable recommendations to support executive decision-making.


## 🎯 Business Objective

To analyse Karoo & Co.'s 2025 sales data, identify meaningful business trends, and provide data-driven recommendations through interactive Business Intelligence dashboards.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Revenue remained consistent throughout 2025.
- Online sales generated strong revenue despite Retail Stores selling more units.
- Returning customers contributed significantly to total revenue.
- Marketing campaigns positively influenced revenue and profitability.
- Product profitability differed from sales volume, highlighting opportunities to optimise pricing and promotions.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing sales channels.
- Strengthen customer retention initiatives.
- Review pricing and discount strategies.
- Continue leveraging Business Intelligence dashboards to support data-driven decision-making.
